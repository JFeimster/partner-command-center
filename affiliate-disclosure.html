Referral and affiliate relationships

Some links, offers, marketplace listings, tools, resources, or partner routes in the Moonshine Partner Command Center may involve referral, affiliate, marketing, or partner compensation. This means Moonshine Capital or a participating partner may receive compensation if a user takes an action, submits information, signs up, purchases a service, or completes a funded transaction through a relationship or link.

Compensation structures may vary and may include flat referral fees, affiliate commissions, revenue share, marketing fees, performance-based compensation, or other partner arrangements.

Compensation does not guarantee suitability

Compensation does not mean an offer is the best fit for every business. Business owners and partners should evaluate options based on the business need, qualifications, costs, terms, risk, documentation, timing, and provider requirements.

Referral or affiliate compensation does not guarantee approval, funding, terms, suitability, payout, or business outcomes.

Partner disclosure expectations

Partners who publish content, send links, host events, run ads, share posts, or make introductions should disclose material compensation relationships where appropriate.

  • Use clear language near links or calls to action.
  • Do not bury disclosures where normal users will not see them.
  • Do not use disclosures to excuse misleading claims.
  • Disclose in the format required by the channel you are using.
  • When in doubt, be clear instead of clever.

Sample disclosure language

Partners can adapt language like the examples below. These examples are not legal advice.

I may receive referral or affiliate compensation if you use this link or take action through this resource. Funding options and eligibility are subject to review and are not guaranteed.
Some resources I share may include partner or referral relationships. Compensation does not affect whether a business is approved or funded.
This content is educational and may include affiliate/referral links. No approval, funding, rate, term, or outcome is guaranteed.

Marketplace listings

Marketplace offers shown in this static system are examples and may include potential partner relationships. Availability, eligibility, pricing, commissions, and terms may change and must be verified before partners promote or rely on them.

Partner links and tracking

Partner links may include identifiers such as partner IDs, referral IDs, affiliate IDs, UTM parameters, or source tags. These identifiers help attribute traffic or referrals in future systems, but the partner workspace does not create live tracking or payout records.

  • Use partner links truthfully.
  • Do not misrepresent what a link does.
  • Do not claim link clicks guarantee eligibility, approval, funding, or payout.
  • Future tracking requires backend validation and partner terms.

Questions and updates

This disclosure may be updated as the partner ecosystem, marketplace, integrations, or compensation structures evolve. Operators should review this page before launching live affiliate or referral campaigns.
