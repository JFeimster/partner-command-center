Core rule

Do not promise outcomes.

Partners may educate, introduce, organize, and route business owners into a review path. Partners should not promise approval, funding, specific rates, terms, timelines, lender matches, commissions, revenue, or risk-free outcomes.

Funding options vary by business profile. Use this workflow to route the next review step clearly.

Safer language to use

Use language that frames funding as a review, readiness, or exploration process.

  • Explore funding options.
  • Review your funding readiness.
  • Prepare documentation for review.
  • Submit information for consideration.
  • Funding options may vary by business profile.
  • Availability, terms, and timelines are subject to review.

Restricted language to avoid

These phrases invite trouble with a megaphone. Do not use them in partner outreach, landing pages, calls, emails, SMS, social posts, or resource copy.

  • Guaranteed funding.
  • Guaranteed approval.
  • Everyone qualifies.
  • No one gets denied.
  • Risk-free funding.
  • Guaranteed commissions.
  • Automatic approval.
  • Earn guaranteed income.

Permission-based referrals

Partners should only submit or share information from business owners who gave permission. This static dashboard is not designed for sensitive data collection.

  • Get permission before submitting contact details.
  • Tell the business owner why information is being shared.
  • Do not submit bank logins, Social Security numbers, full tax IDs, account numbers, private documents, or sensitive borrower records into this partner workspace.
  • Use the demo form for workflow testing, not live underwriting.

Partner responsibility

Partners are responsible for truthful, respectful, and permission-based promotion. If a partner publishes content, sends messages, runs ads, speaks at events, or makes introductions, the partner should use accurate disclosures and avoid misleading claims.

  • Represent the partner program honestly.
  • Disclose material affiliate or referral relationships where appropriate.
  • Do not imply Moonshine Capital has approved a business before review.
  • Do not describe estimated commissions as guaranteed payouts.
  • Do not present educational tools as underwriting decisions.

Static dashboard disclaimer

The Partner Command Center currently uses sample data and browser localStorage for demo workflows. It does not create live partner accounts, live lead records, live CRM entries, live lender submissions, or live commission records.

Partner workspace only.

Local data may be deleted if the browser storage is cleared. Future backend integrations must add validation, consent capture, authentication, access controls, audit logs, and secure storage.

Operator review standards

Internal operators should review partner copy, marketplace offers, scripts, and integration workflows before treating them as production-ready.

  • Check every CTA for misleading urgency.
  • Check every payout reference for no-guarantee language.
  • Check every lead workflow for consent and privacy gaps.
  • Check every tool for educational-only positioning.
  • Check every integration example for clear example-only labeling.
