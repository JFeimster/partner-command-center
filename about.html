About the ecosystem

Partner growth needs infrastructure, not another pile of links in a trench coat.

Moonshine Partner Command Center is the static-first front-end layer for a partner-powered funding ecosystem: acquisition pages, access flow, dashboard, marketplace, resources, tools, widgets, admin prototypes, and future integration docs.

Join the Partner Program Explore Marketplace
Operating belief

Underdogs need better rails.

The goal is to help self-starters, operators, brokers, affiliates, and community builders route opportunity without relying on chaos, fake promises, or duct-taped spreadsheets.

Mission

Turn partner attention into partner action.

The Command Center gives partners a place to understand the program, create a profile, submit demo leads, build partner links, browse resources, complete training, and prepare for future integrated workflows.

Public

Acquire partners

Explain the program, preview the value, and route visitors into partner access.

Partner

Enable execution

Give partners a dashboard, scripts, training, links, lead tracking, and resource paths.

Operator

Prepare integration

Use static prototypes to plan CRM, webhooks, widgets, GPT actions, admin flows, and APIs.

Future roadmap

Static today. Backend-ready tomorrow.

The repo starts with localStorage and mock data so the workflows can be reviewed fast. Future phases can connect real authentication, CRM, forms, widgets, APIs, and operator workflows.

Current static layer

  • Public acquisition pages
  • Partner access flow
  • Local dashboard state
  • Mock leads and commissions
  • Marketplace and resources data
  • Compliance-safe copy library

Future integrated layer

  • Partner accounts and auth
  • Lead submission API
  • Tally and CRM webhooks
  • Partner attribution service
  • Embeddable widgets
  • Admin review and reporting
Important disclosure: Moonshine Partner Command Center is not a lender decision engine, underwriting system, legal advisor, tax advisor, or guaranteed income program. It is a static-first partner workflow system and future integration blueprint.
