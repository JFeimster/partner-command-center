CRM blueprint

Recommended CRM objects

The future CRM layer should separate partners, leads, events, resources, and attribution records instead of cramming everything into one haunted spreadsheet.

Partner

Partner identity, partner type, status, approved channels, disclosure acknowledgment, and partner ID.

Lead

Business basics, contact details, funding need, use of funds, status, partner ID, consent, and source.

Activity Event

Profile created, lead submitted, status updated, resource saved, link copied, widget submitted.

Offer / Resource

Marketplace category, compliance note, allowed partner types, CTA route, and status.

Sync rules

  • Do not sync static demo records into production CRM by accident.
  • Validate partner ID before creating a live lead.
  • Capture consent source, timestamp, page URL, and partner ID.
  • Use immutable event logs for status changes.
  • Do not allow partners to overwrite underwriting or provider status fields directly.
  • Keep commission status separate from lead status.
